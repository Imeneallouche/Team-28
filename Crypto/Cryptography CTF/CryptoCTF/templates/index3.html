In the previous game, we used the verb "to encrypt". What's that?

Encryption is securing information by transforming it into a form that only intended recipients can read and process.

It's like having a secret language that only you and the receiver of your message can understand, so you use it to protect your messages from being infiltrated.

There are many "secret languages" that are commonly used. A very common one of them is the "Caesar cipher", in which you shift the letters a certain number of time in a certain direction.

For example, if we shift right with 3 positions, A becomes D, B becomes E ... and Z becomes C.
