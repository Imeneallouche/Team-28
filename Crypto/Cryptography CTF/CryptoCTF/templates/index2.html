Cryptography is the process of coding or hiding a message in a way that only the recipient of the message can read it.

For example, if you receive this message :

.... . .-.. .-.. --- / .-- --- .-. .-.. -..

Once you figure out that it's "encrypted" in what we call "Morse code", you can easily figure out what's written!
